AI Suggested Answer

To verify tester engagement, you should check the Closed Testing track in your Google Play Console. Look specifically for the "Opt-in" numbers.

Key steps:

  • Ensure your testers have installed the app and opened it daily.
  • Google requires 14 continuous days. If engagement drops, the timer might reset.
  • Use our Dashboard > Reports tab to download proof of engagement logs for your appeal.
